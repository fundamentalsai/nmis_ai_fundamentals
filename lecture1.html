Chapter 5: Training Neural Networks and Loss Function

This chapter explores the processes involved in training neural networks, emphasizing the role of loss functions. It covers how neural networks learn from data, the importance of choosing appropriate loss functions, and techniques to minimize loss for better model accuracy and performance. For instance, imagine trying to create the perfect drink for yourself. Each attempt (or "training iteration") involves mixing different ingredients. After tasting, you evaluate how close it is to your ideal drink by giving it a score (similar to calculating loss). The goal is to adjust the mix (or "model parameters") to minimize the score, ultimately creating the perfect drink.
