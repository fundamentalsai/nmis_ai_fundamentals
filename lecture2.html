Video Chapters

Chapter 1: Foundations of Neural Network Structures

This chapter transitions from introductory discussions to the technical aspects of artificial neural networks (ANNs). It emphasizes the importance of practical experience with coding and data manipulation, starting with a hand-drawing exercise to review basic neural network structures. This foundational review prepares learners for more complex topics later in the course.

Watch Chapter 1

Chapter 2: Introduction to Matrix Operations in Neural Networks

This chapter delves into the fundamental matrix operations essential for understanding neural networks. It covers how to correctly multiply matrices and vectors, emphasizing the order of operations and the role of weights and biases in these calculations. learners are guided through practical exercises to solidify their grasp of these concepts, preparing them for more advanced neural network structures and functions.

Watch Chapter 2

Chapter 3:Training Neural Networks with Google Colab workshop

This chapter focuses on the initial steps of training a neural network model using a prewritten Google Colab exercise. It begins with setting random values for weights and biases and then introduces the process of feeding input data into the model. The goal is to adjust these weights and biases based on the outputs to improve model predictions. Learners engage in practical exercises, modifying code and inputs within Google Colab to observe the effects on the network's predictions. This hands-on approach helps solidify the understanding of neural network training principles.Workshop Exercise: The workshop exercise corresponding to this chapter can be found above under "Workshop"

Watch Chapter 3

Chapter 4: Neural Activation Functions and Normalization

In this chapter, the focus shifts to understanding the role of activation functions in neural networks and the process of normalization. The discussion delves into how the weighted sum calculated by neurons is transformed using activation functions to produce an output within a specific range. Additionally, the concept of normalization is introduced to scale the outputs to a desired range, ensuring consistency in model performance. Through practical examples and explanations, learners learn how to apply activation functions and normalization techniques effectively in neural network training.
